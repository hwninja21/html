This is because insurers’ appetite for risk is now much more selective. However, it is in these challenging markets that the value of a specialist insurance broker is even more evident.
At London Belgravia Brokers, our first step is to review your existing insurance plan to ensure you have adequate cover in place. This will allow us to identify any gaps and identify new risk exposures. As one of the largest specialist risk brokers in the market, we are able to leverage our scale and relationships in the industry to solve problems for our clients and obtain a suitable cover – effectively mitigating the impact of the hard market.
We offer cover for both contractors and developers, for CAR, Public Liability, Employers Liability, Property Owners Liability, Plant and Tools, Terrorism and Delay in start-up among many others. Also, we only work with A-Rated insurers and ensure a quick turn around time for your quote – allowing you to switch providers promptly if required. We can quote on project basis as well as annual policies.
In addition, we can support our client base with other services including latent defects insurance, performance bonds, Insurance backed guarantees – all of which are linked and in the long term could help you reduce your risk and premium.
